OpenAI Retiring GPT-4 and GPT-4.5 Models

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

OpenAI announced they're retiring GPT-4 (released spring 2023) on April 30th, replacing it with GPT-4o. They're also phasing out GPT-4.5, which was released in late February 2025 and was known for creative writing and human-like chat capabilities despite not being the strongest for complex logic, math, and reasoning.
