OpenAI Releases O3 and O4 Mini 'Thinking' Models

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

OpenAI released O3 and O4 Mini, new 'thinking' models that reason through problems before responding. These models can analyze images during reasoning, integrate them into their thought process, and use tools like web search and Python code execution multiple times during thinking. They show impressive performance on math benchmarks, with O4 Mini with Python scoring 99.5% on 2025 competition math. According to reports, these models may be capable of generating novel ideas by synthesizing information from multiple fields simultaneously.
