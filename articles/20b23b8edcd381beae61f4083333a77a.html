Reddit sues Anthropic over data scraping

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

Reddit is suing Anthropic for allegedly accessing Reddit more than 100,000 times since last year to train its AI models. Reddit has a data-sharing agreement with Google (which owns 14% of Anthropic) but does not have a similar arrangement with Anthropic directly. The lawsuit claims Anthropic bypassed Reddit's APIs to scrape information without permission.
