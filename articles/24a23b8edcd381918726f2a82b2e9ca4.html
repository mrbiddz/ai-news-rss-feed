Google DeepMind Unveils Genie 3

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

Google DeepMind has unveiled Genie 3, a frontier world model that can generate interactive environments in real-time. The technology allows users to navigate various scenarios like the surface of the moon, volcanoes, coastal cliffs, underwater environments, and more. Users can control movement within these generated environments using arrow keys, with every frame being generated on the fly. Genie 3 can also take existing videos generated with other tools like VO3 and allow users to take control of the video midway through. The technology is currently available to early testers.
