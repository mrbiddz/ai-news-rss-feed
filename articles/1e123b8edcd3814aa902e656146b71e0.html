Perplexity Rolls Out Assistant Feature in iOS App

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Perplexity has rolled out a new feature called Perplexity Assistant in their iOS app. The assistant can perform tasks like playing media, drafting emails, moving meetings, booking rides, making reservations, and setting reminders. It can open podcasts (in Apple Podcasts), book reservations through Open Table, and check calendar events. While some features work well, others are still described as 'janky' by Perplexity's CEO Raven.
