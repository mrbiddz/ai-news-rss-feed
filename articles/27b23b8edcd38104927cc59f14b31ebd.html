Notebook LM Adds Flashcards and Quizzes

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Google's Notebook LM has rolled out flashcards and quizzes features to all users in all markets. These educational tools complement existing features like audio overviews, video overviews, mind maps, and reports. Users can upload documents and have the AI automatically generate flashcards and quizzes based on the content.
