ChatGPT Introduces Study Mode

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

OpenAI introduced a Study Mode in ChatGPT designed to help students learn rather than just providing answers. When given problems like math equations, it guides users through the solution process step by step instead of immediately giving the final answer, making it more suitable for homework help and test preparation.
