Google Launches Gemini 2.5 Flash

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Google released Gemini 2.5 Flash, a lighter and faster model than Gemini 2.5. It's their first fully hybrid reasoning model where developers can turn thinking on or off. The model is less expensive than competitors like O4 Mini and Claude Sonnet 3.7 when reasoning is off, though prices increase when reasoning is enabled. It performs well in science, mathematics, code, and visual reasoning, and ranks highly in user preference testing on LM Arena while maintaining competitive pricing.
