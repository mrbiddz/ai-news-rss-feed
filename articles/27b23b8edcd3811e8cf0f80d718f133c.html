Photoshop Beta Integrates Nano Banana

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Adobe has integrated Nano Banana into Photoshop Beta, allowing users to choose between Firefly Image 3, Lux Context Pro, and Nano Banana for generative fill operations. The integration maintains Photoshop's non-destructive workflow by creating new layers for AI-generated content.
