Google Unveils Veo 3 Video Model

Published: Sun, 25 May 2025

Google introduced Veo 3, their new video model that significantly improves video quality while adding dialogue, sound effects, and background music. The model is so realistic that some videos have fooled people into thinking they're real. Veo 3 features impressive lip-syncing and visual quality, though it has some issues with physics and randomly adds subtitles with typos. Currently, Veo 3 is only available through Google AI Ultra plan at $250/month (currently 50% off), but users face strict rate limits of only five generations before being cut off until the next day.
