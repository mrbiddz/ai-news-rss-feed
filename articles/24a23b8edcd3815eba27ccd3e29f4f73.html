Grock Launches Image and Video Generation Feature

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

Grock has launched a new feature called Grock Imagine that can create images and videos with audio, similar to VO3. The feature is extremely fast and is available inside the Grock app for Super Grock and Premium Plus subscribers. Elon Musk announced that for a limited time, Grock Imagine Video Generation would be free to all US users, suggesting that non-US users could access it using a VPN. The tool allows users to generate videos from existing images with different modes including normal, fun, spicy (NSFW), and custom.
