11 Labs Releases Sound Effects Model Version 2

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

11 Labs has released version 2 of their sound effects model with improved quality and a new seamless looping feature. This allows users to generate continuous sound effects like a crackling fireplace that can loop indefinitely without noticeable breaks.
