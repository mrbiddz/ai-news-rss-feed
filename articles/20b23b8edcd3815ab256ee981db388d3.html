Multiple AI avatar and video generation tools released

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

Several companies released or updated AI avatar and video generation tools this week. HeyGen upgraded their Avatar 4 to create more realistic AI avatars with improved lip-syncing. Captions launched a competing product with more emotive characters and better audio, though with some video quality issues. Higsfield AI, a rapidly growing company, added lip-syncing capabilities to their special effects platform. Additionally, Menace released a new video generation tool that appears to match the quality of existing options like Sora.
