Google Updates AI Search and Introduces New Language Tools

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Google has made their AI mode available to all Labs users in the US, with plans to roll it out to a small percentage of non-Labs users in the coming weeks. The feature provides AI-generated responses to search queries with links to sources, similar to Perplexity and ChatGPT search. Google also added image editing capabilities to the Gemini app on Android, allowing users to upload and edit images directly in the app. Notebook LM now supports audio overviews in over 50 languages, and Google launched Little Language Lessons, a new app with three experiments to help travelers learn essential phrases: Tiny Lesson for vocabulary and grammar, Slang Hang for learning expressions from generated conversations between native speakers, and Word Cam for learning about surroundings through photos.
