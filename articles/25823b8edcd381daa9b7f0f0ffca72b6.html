Runway ML Launches Game Worlds Beta

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Runway ML has launched Game Worlds beta, a new feature that creates personalized text-based adventure games with AI-generated images. Available at play.runwayml.com, users can create game worlds by naming them and customizing genres, art styles, and game mechanics. The experience resembles a comic book with text and images generated in real-time as players make choices throughout their adventure.
