Juan 2.5 Open-Source Video Model Added to Platforms

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Juan 2.5, widely regarded as the best open-source AI video model, has been added to platforms like Higsfield AI and Craya. The model can generate both video and audio, is comparable to Runway's Gen-3, and can be run locally on sufficiently powerful computers.
