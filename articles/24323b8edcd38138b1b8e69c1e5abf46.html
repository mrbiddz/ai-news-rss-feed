Meshy 5 Released with Improved 3D Model Generation

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Meshy released version 5 of their 3D model generation tool, which creates smarter and cleaner 3D models from text or image inputs. The tool can generate detailed 3D models with textures from simple prompts or convert 2D images into 3D models with impressive accuracy, even for complex scenes.
