Perplexity Partners with Coinbase for Crypto Data

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

Perplexity has partnered with Coinbase to provide more comprehensive cryptocurrency data directly within its platform. Users can access crypto prices and data from spot transactions on Coinbase by navigating to the Finance section in Perplexity's sidebar and selecting the Crypto tab.
