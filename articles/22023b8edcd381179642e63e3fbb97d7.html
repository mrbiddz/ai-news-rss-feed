Meta Wins AI Copyright Case on Technicality

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Meta won a copyright case related to AI training, though the judge noted it was due to the plaintiffs making the wrong arguments. The plaintiffs claimed Meta's Llama could regurgitate entire books, making it unnecessary for people to buy them, but they couldn't prove this in court. The judge clarified that the ruling doesn't necessarily mean Meta's use of copyrighted materials for training is lawful.
