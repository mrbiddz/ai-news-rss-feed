Seeddream 4.0 Launches as Nano Banana Competitor

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

ByteDance has released Seeddream 4.0, a new image editing model positioned as a competitor to Nano Banana. Similar to Nano Banana, it allows users to edit images by describing desired changes or combining multiple images. Currently available through f.ai, it costs about 3 cents per image, while Nano Banana remains free in AI Studio.
