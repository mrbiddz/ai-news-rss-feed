Google Launches Storybook Feature in Gemini App

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

Google has rolled out a new feature in the Gemini app that allows users to create personal illustrated storybooks. Users can specify details like the reader's age, illustration style, and tone to generate custom stories with consistent artwork throughout. The feature can create both child-friendly stories and more adult-oriented content, generating approximately 10-page books with illustrations that maintain character consistency throughout the narrative.
