Google's Notebook LM Video Overview Feature Now Available

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Google's Notebook LM has rolled out its video overview feature to all users. This tool allows users to upload various sources (articles, websites, YouTube videos) and generate an explainer video based on the content. The feature creates a slideshow-style video with voiceover that resembles a podcast. The quality is impressive, suggesting it uses Google Vids rather than Google's V3 video model.
