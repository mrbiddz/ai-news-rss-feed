11 Labs Launches Mobile App

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

11 Labs released a new mobile app that enables text-to-speech directly from phones. The app allows users to add text, change voices, and generate audio. Users can also view the history of past audio chats from both the web app and mobile app.
