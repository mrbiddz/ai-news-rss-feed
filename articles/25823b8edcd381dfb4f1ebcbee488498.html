Meta Expected to Reveal New Smart Glasses with Display

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Meta is expected to showcase new smart glasses at Meta Connect this year, rumored to feature a display in one lens that provides a heads-up display for apps and alerts. The glasses are expected to be priced around $800 and include a wrist accessory to help control them.
