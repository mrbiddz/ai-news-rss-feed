Google Photos Adding Photo-to-Video Conversion

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Google Photos is rolling out an update that allows users to turn their photos into videos. Using the 'I'm feeling lucky' button, the app can generate a video from a still image using V2 technology. According to Google, this feature is starting to roll out in the US on Android and iOS. Additionally, Google Photos is adding a remix feature that lets users restylize photos in different styles like cartoon or anime.
