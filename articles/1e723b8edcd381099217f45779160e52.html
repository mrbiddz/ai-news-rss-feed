MidJourney Launches Omni Reference Feature

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

MidJourney rolled out a new feature called Omni Reference that allows users to incorporate specific elements from reference images into their generated images. The feature works with characters, objects, vehicles, and more. Users can upload reference images, adjust the strength of the reference using a slider, and combine them with text prompts to create customized images that maintain characteristics from the reference.
