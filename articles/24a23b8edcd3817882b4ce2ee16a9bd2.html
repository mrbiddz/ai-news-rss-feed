Google's Kaggle Launches AI Chess Tournament

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

Kaggle, a Google company, is organizing a series of game tournaments to test different AI models against each other. The first event is a three-day AI chess tournament in partnership with chess.com to evaluate which models are currently best at chess. This is the first in a planned series of tournaments that will use various strategic games to assess AI models' thinking and reasoning capabilities.
