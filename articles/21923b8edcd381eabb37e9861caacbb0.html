Google Introduces Search Live Feature

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

Google has launched a new feature called Search Live that allows users to have conversations with Google Search. Available in the Google app on both iPhone and Android, users can ask questions and receive audio responses based on Google search results. The feature performs searches automatically and synthesizes information from multiple sources to provide answers.
