Adobe Acrobat Introduces PDF Spaces for AI-Powered Document Interaction

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Adobe Acrobat has introduced PDF Spaces, a new feature that allows users to have conversations with their PDFs. The tool extracts information from PDFs and presents it in an organized format. Users can choose different AI assistants (analyst, entertainer, or instructor) to interact with the content in different ways, making it easier to work with long or technical documents.
