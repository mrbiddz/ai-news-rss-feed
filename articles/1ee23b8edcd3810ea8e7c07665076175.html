Google Releases Improved Gemini 2.5 Pro for Coding

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Google released a new version of Gemini 2.5 Pro that ranks as the best coding model available according to benchmarks and LM Arena. One of the new features is video-to-code, where Gemini can understand video content (not just transcribe audio) and generate code based on what it sees. Examples include transforming images into code-based representations of natural behaviors, such as creating interactive trees with growth sliders, spiderwebs, bonfires, and particle simulators. The model is available for free at AI Studio.
