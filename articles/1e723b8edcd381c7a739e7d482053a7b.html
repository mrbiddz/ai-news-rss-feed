Versep Introduces VI, an AI Agent That Operates Your Computer

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Versep announced VI, an AI tool that can see and use your computer just like you do. It runs natively with access to all applications and accounts you're signed into. VI can perform tasks by understanding application interfaces and executing actions even if the user doesn't know how to use the software. For example, it can operate complex programs like Adobe Podcast audio enhancer or Figma by interpreting the UI and clicking the right buttons. While the software can be downloaded, users must join a waiting list to gain access.
