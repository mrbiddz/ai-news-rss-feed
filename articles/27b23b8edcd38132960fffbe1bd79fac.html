Halo AI Launches Creative Agent

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Halo AI has introduced an agent that helps with creative workflows on an infinite canvas. The agent can select the best models for different tasks, plan workflows, and create images, with video and audio capabilities coming soon. It features auto-selection of appropriate models based on prompts and can create mood boards with both searched and AI-generated images.
