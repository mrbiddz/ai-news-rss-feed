Elon Musk Announces Grock 3.5 Coming Next Week

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Elon Musk announced that Grock 3.5 will be available next week for Super Grok subscribers (the highest tier plan at approximately $57/month). According to Musk, Grock 3.5 will be the first AI that can accurately answer technical questions about rocket engines or electrochemistry, reasoning from first principles and providing answers that don't exist on the internet.
