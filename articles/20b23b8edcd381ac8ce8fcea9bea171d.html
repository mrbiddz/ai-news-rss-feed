Drama between OpenAI, Anthropic, and Windsurf

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

Tension emerged between AI companies when Anthropic cut off Windsurf's access to Claude 3.x models with less than 5 days notice. This occurred shortly after reports that OpenAI was acquiring Windsurf. Verun Mohan, CEO of Windsurf, expressed disappointment at the decision and noted they were willing to pay for the full capacity. The situation is complicated by the fact that Dario Amodei, Anthropic's CEO, previously worked at OpenAI.
