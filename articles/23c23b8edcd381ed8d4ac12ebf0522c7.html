Amazon Acquires B AI Wearable

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Amazon has purchased B, an AI wearable device that listens to conversations, transcribes them, and allows users to chat with the transcripts later. The device is particularly useful for conferences and meetings, though there are questions about whether Amazon will maintain the same privacy standards as the original company.
