ChatGPT Introduces Record Mode

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

OpenAI released a new record mode for ChatGPT that allows users to record Zoom calls, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams meetings, and other conversations. ChatGPT will help summarize the calls and extract to-dos after the meeting. The feature records both sides of a conversation and provides a summary rather than a complete transcription. This feature is currently available on paid plans but may eventually come to the free plan.
