Google's Isomorphic Labs Prepares AI-Discovered Drugs for Human Trials

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

Isomorphic Labs, an AI-powered drug company owned by Google, has developed drugs using artificial intelligence that are now ready for human trials. This represents a significant milestone in AI's application to healthcare and drug discovery, showing how AI can make meaningful contributions to medical advancement.
