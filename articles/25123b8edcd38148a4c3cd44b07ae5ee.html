Perplexity Adds Video Generation Feature

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Perplexity now offers video generation capabilities on web, iOS, and Android platforms. Pro subscribers get five video generations per month, while Max subscribers receive 15 generations with better quality. The feature allows users to generate videos from text prompts or images, complete with sound. The generated videos are approximately 8 seconds long, suggesting it might be using Google's V3 technology.
