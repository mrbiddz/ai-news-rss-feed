Alibaba Unveils Quinn 3 Hybrid AI Reasoning Model

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Alibaba introduced Quinn 3, a hybrid AI reasoning model that allows users to toggle chain-of-thought thinking on or off. When enabled, the model provides more accurate responses by showing its thought process, but takes longer. When disabled, responses are faster but potentially less accurate. According to benchmarks, Quinn 3 performs well, particularly in coding and competition math.
