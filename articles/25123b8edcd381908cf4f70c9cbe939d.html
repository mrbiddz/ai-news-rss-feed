Figure Robotics Unveils Laundry-Folding Robot

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Figure Robotics demonstrated a robot that can fold laundry. This follows their previous demonstration of a robot that could transfer laundry between machines. The Figure robot can now handle the complete laundry process - loading and unloading machines as well as folding clothes.
