AI Talent Poaching Intensifies Between Tech Giants

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

Major AI companies continue to poach talent from each other with substantial compensation packages. Meta has recruited Ruming Pang, one of Apple's top AI executives who was in charge of Apple's foundation models team, reportedly offering a package worth tens of millions of dollars per year. Meanwhile, OpenAI has hired several executives from competitors, including David Laauo (former VP of software engineering at Tesla), Uday (former head of infrastructure engineering at XAI), Mike Dalton (infrastructure engineer from XAI), and Angela Fan (AI researcher from Meta).
