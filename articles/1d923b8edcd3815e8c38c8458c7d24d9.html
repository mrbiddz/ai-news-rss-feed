Cling Launches 2.0 Video Generation Model

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Cling released version 2.0 of their AI video generation model, introducing multimodal visual language that enables users to convey complex creative ideas through image references and video clips. The new model shows improvements in action adherence, camera movements, sequential actions, motion dynamics, visual style consistency, and expressions. Users have shared impressive examples including realistic jet flights, cinematic scenes, and the ability to swap actors in existing film scenes.
