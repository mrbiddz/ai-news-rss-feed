Meta Expands AI Translation for Reels

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Meta is expanding their AI translation feature to automatically translate Instagram and Facebook Reels. The technology uses the sound and tone of the creator's own voice and includes lip-syncing that matches mouth movements to the translated audio, making it appear as if the person is actually speaking the translated language.
