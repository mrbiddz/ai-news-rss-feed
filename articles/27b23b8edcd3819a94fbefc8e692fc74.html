Mirage (Formerly Captions) Launches Text-Based Video Editing

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Mirage, formerly known as Captions, has launched a text-based video editing feature in their mobile app. Users can make edits by typing commands like "zoom at 3 seconds" or requesting captions. The app also allows users to specify a vibe for AI to apply the right style and aesthetic. However, the feature has limitations including requiring 9:16 aspect ratio videos and detecting speech in videos.
