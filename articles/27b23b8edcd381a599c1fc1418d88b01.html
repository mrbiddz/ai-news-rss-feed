Google Integrates Gemini into Chrome

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Google has integrated Gemini directly into Chrome for Mac and Windows desktop users in the US. The feature adds a Gemini logo in the top right corner of the browser and allows users to ask questions about the current page, get key points highlighted, or discuss content with the AI. Users can switch between Gemini 2.5 Pro and 2.5 Flash models.
