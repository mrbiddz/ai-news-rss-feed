Pika Labs Launching Social AI Video App

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Pika Labs is rolling out a new invite-only app called Pika Social AI video. The app allows users to take selfies and then animate them or place themselves in different AI-generated scenes, creating a social media feed of AI-enhanced versions of real people.
