GLM 4.5: Impressive New Open Weight Model

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

GLM 4.5 is a new open weight model that performs almost as well as GPT-4 and Claude 4 Opus on benchmarks, particularly excelling at agentic reasoning and coding. Available for free at Z.ai, it can create impressive slide decks with images and formatting, and can one-shot code complex applications like a Vampire Survivors clone in JavaScript. The model is extremely fast and completely free to use on their website.
