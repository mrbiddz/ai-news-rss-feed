Getty Drops Part of Copyright Claim Against Stability AI

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Getty Images dropped a portion of its copyright claim against Stability AI after seeing the favorable rulings that Anthropic and Meta received. Getty was suing Stability for using its images and generating similar images. They specifically dropped the portion related to the fair use of training data, though they didn't drop the entire case.
