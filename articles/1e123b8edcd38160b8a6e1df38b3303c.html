New AI APIs for Developers

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Several new APIs were released for developers this week. OpenAI shipped their image generation model in the API, making the technology behind ChatGPT's image generation available to developers. There's also a new Grok 3 Mini API, which according to benchmarks outperforms Gemini 2.5 Flash, O4 Mini High, Deepseek R1, and even Claude 3.7 Sonnet Thinking model at a lower price point.
