Sam Altman Appears on New York Times Podcast Amid Lawsuit

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Sam Altman appeared on the Hard Fork podcast, which is owned by the New York Times - a company currently suing OpenAI. The New York Times is trying to get OpenAI to share their data and save customer user data for potential future discovery in court. The interview was described as awkward, with the host making pointed comments about data usage.
