Notebook LM Adds New Audio Overview Options

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Google's Notebook LM has expanded its audio overview feature with new options beyond the default deep dive. Users can now choose from a brief (1-2 minute overview), a critique (expert review with constructive feedback), or a debate mode (thoughtful debate between two hosts). These features are available to all users regardless of subscription plan.
