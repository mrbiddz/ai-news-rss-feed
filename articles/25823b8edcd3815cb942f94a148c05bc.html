Quinn Image Edit: New AI Image Editor from Alibaba

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Quinn Image Edit is a new AI image editor from Chinese company Alibaba, released under the Apache 2.0 license. Similar to ChatGPT's image editing capabilities, it allows users to modify images with text prompts. Examples include changing clothing, adjusting viewing angles, altering styles, and adding elements to images. The model is available for free at quin.ai, where users can upload images and describe desired edits.
