Hey Gen Launches Avatar 4 for AI Video Creation

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Hey Gen released Avatar 4, a new feature that allows users to upload a single photo along with a script and voice recording to create an AI avatar talking head video. The technology uses a diffusion-inspired audio-to-expression engine that analyzes vocal tone, rhythm, and emotion to synthesize photorealistic facial motion with temporal realism, including head tilts, pauses, cadences, and micro-expressions. The feature works with profile images and can create talking animals, game characters, and anime characters.
