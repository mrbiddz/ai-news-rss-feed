OpenAI Rolls Back ChatGPT-4o Personality Update

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

OpenAI rolled back recent updates to GPT-4o after Sam Altman expressed dissatisfaction with the model becoming too 'sycophantic' or overly flattering and agreeable. The company acknowledged they had put too much focus on short-term feedback without fully accounting for how user interactions evolve over time. OpenAI is now revising how they collect and incorporate feedback to prioritize long-term user satisfaction. Additionally, ChatGPT received several feature updates including improved search functionality, a better shopping experience with product carousels, web search in WhatsApp, improved citations, and trending topics with autocomplete in the prompt window.
