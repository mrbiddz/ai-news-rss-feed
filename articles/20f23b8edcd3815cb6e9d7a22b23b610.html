Privacy Concerns with AR Glasses

Published: Thu, 12 Jun 2025

Regarding Android XR glasses with cameras and microphones, Hassabis addressed privacy concerns by emphasizing the importance of making it obvious when devices are on or off through user interfaces and form factors. He acknowledged that society needs to develop agreements and norms about how these devices should be used if they become popular and useful. Hassabis noted that this is partly why they're still in the trusted tester phase - not only is the technology still developing, but they need to think about societal impacts and establish appropriate guardrails ahead of time.
