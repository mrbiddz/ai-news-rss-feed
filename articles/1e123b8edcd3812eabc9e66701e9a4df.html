Washington Post Partners with OpenAI for Search Content

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

The Washington Post has partnered with OpenAI to allow the AI company to search Washington Post content when users use OpenAI's search features. This appears to be part of a trend where OpenAI is making partnership deals with various news outlets, potentially to minimize lawsuits as they integrate more news content into their services.
