Sam Altman Believes We're in an AI Bubble

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman believes we're currently in an AI bubble, comparing it to the tech bubble of the early 2000s. While he thinks investors are overexcited about AI, he also believes AI is 'the most important thing to happen in a very long time.' He suggests valuations may crash, but like the internet after its bubble burst, AI will remain significant with some big winners emerging.
