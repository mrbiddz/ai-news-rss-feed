Google Flow Platform for Filmmakers

Published: Sun, 25 May 2025

Google announced Flow, a new platform designed for filmmakers that allows users to create and edit AI-generated videos. The platform offers text-to-video, frames-to-video, and ingredients-to-video features. Users can create video timelines, extend existing videos, and transform scenes. While still in early beta with some functionality issues, Flow shows promising potential for creative video production workflows.
