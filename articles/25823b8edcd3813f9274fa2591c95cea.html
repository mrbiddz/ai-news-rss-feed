11 Labs Introduces Video to Music Feature

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

11 Labs has launched a new video to music feature in 11 Lab Studio that automatically generates custom soundtracks based on video context. The system analyzes video content and creates appropriate music to match the mood and style. After adding music, users can layer in voiceovers and sound effects directly in the studio. The feature is available at 11labs.io/studio/videotomusic.
