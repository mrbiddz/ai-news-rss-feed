Recall Offers AI-Powered Memory for Digital Content

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Recall is an AI-powered tool that helps users save, summarize, and connect everything they read, watch, or listen to online. It features a browser extension that can summarize articles, YouTube videos, and podcasts with one click. The tool automatically tags, organizes, and connects content in a knowledge base, and allows users to chat across everything they've saved. It can also import past notes, documents, bookmarks, and social media content.
