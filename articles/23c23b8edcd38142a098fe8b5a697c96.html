Proton Launches Lumo, a Private AI Chatbot

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Proton, known for their email service, has launched Lumo, a confidential chatbot that keeps conversations completely private. The service doesn't collect data, and even Proton cannot access chat history. The free plan includes web search capabilities, limited weekly chats, and small file uploads. A Plus plan is available for $9.99/month with unlimited chats and advanced models.
