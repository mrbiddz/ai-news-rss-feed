Crea Launches New Image Generator

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

Crea has released a new AI image generator called Crea 1, developed in collaboration with Black Forest Labs. The model is expected to be on par with Black Forest Labs' Flux models, providing users with another option in the growing field of AI image generation tools.
