OpenAI extends memory feature to free users and adds connectors

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

OpenAI rolled out its memory feature to free ChatGPT users, allowing the AI to learn from past conversations and use that context in new chats. Additionally, OpenAI introduced connectors for Plus and Pro users, enabling ChatGPT to access information from services like Outlook, Microsoft Teams, Google Drive, Gmail, SharePoint, Dropbox, and Box.
