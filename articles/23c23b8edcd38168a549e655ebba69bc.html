White House Releases AI Policy Recommendations

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

The White House has released a 28-page document outlining its AI policy recommendations. The focus is on accelerating AI innovation by removing red tape, protecting free speech, encouraging open source models, building American AI infrastructure (data centers, semiconductor manufacturing, energy infrastructure), and leading in international AI diplomacy.
