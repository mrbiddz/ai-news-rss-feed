AI Creating New AI Algorithms

Published: Thu, 12 Jun 2025

Google DeepMind recently announced Alpha Evolve, an AI system that can help develop new algorithms. Hassabis described it as a breakthrough that combines evolutionary methods with LLMs to invent something new. He believes there's promising work ahead in combining different computer science methods with foundation models like Gemini. Hassabis clarified that the system still has humans in the loop and scientists overseeing the process - it's not directly improving Gemini but using these techniques to improve the AI ecosystem around it, creating slightly better algorithms and chips that the system trains on.
