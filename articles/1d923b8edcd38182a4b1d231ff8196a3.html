Crisp Introduces Accent Conversion Technology

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Crisp, known for background noise removal, now offers accent conversion technology. The feature can modify accents while preserving the speaker's original voice, potentially allowing call centers to make representatives sound like they have local accents.
