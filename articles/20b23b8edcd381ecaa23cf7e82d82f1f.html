Luma AI launches new video modification feature

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

Luma AI released a new 'modify video' feature that allows users to upload their own videos and reimagine them in different styles. The most impressive aspect is the ability to keep characters consistent while changing their surroundings or outfits. While the company's demos appear impressive, user results have been mixed, suggesting the feature may require specific prompting techniques to achieve optimal results.
