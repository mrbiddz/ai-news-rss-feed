HeyGen Launches Avatar 4 for More Realistic Digital Twins

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

HeyGen has released Avatar 4, an upgrade to their digital twin technology that claims to mirror users' gestures, expressions, and mannerisms more accurately. The new version shows improved lip-syncing and fewer visual abnormalities, particularly with facial hair which was problematic in previous versions. However, the default AI voice still sounds robotic, though users can integrate with 11 Labs for potentially better voice quality.
