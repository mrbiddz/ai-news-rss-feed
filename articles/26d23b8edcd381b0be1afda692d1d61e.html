Microsoft's Complex AI Partnerships: OpenAI and Anthropic

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Microsoft, which owns approximately 49% of OpenAI, is now looking to buy AI from Anthropic (an OpenAI competitor). Simultaneously, Microsoft and OpenAI released a joint statement confirming they've signed a non-binding memorandum of understanding for the next phase of their partnership. Microsoft is also investing in building its own in-house AI models.
