Quinn Models Get Ultra-Long Context Windows

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Alibaba's Quinn models (Quinn 3B A3B 257 and Quinn 3235B A22B 257) received updates giving them ultra-long context windows of up to 1 million tokens. This follows the trend of models getting increasingly large context windows, potentially making context window size less of a differentiating factor in the future.
