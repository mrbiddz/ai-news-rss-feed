Unitree Announces R1 Consumer Robot for $5,900

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Unitree announced the R1 robot, a consumer-grade humanoid robot priced at $5,900. While relatively affordable for a humanoid robot, it currently appears more focused on demonstrations like cartwheels and handstands rather than practical household tasks.
