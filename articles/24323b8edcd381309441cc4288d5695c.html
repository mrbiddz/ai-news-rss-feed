Amazon Invests in Fable, the 'Netflix of AI'

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Amazon invested in Fable, a company positioning itself as the 'Netflix of AI' that generates full-length (20-30 minute) cartoon episodes based on user prompts. The company plans to eventually license IP from major studios like Disney and Universal to allow users to generate new episodes in existing franchises.
