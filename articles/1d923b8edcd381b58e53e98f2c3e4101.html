OpenAI Launches GPT-4.1 Models

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

OpenAI rolled out GPT-4.1, 4.1 Mini, and 4.1 Nano, currently available only in the API. Unlike the 'thinking' models, these provide immediate responses. They're smarter than previous models, particularly at coding, though not as good at instruction following as GPT-4.5. A key advantage is the massive 1 million token context window (approximately 750,000 words). The pricing is significantly lower at $1.84 per million tokens compared to GPT-4.5's previous $75-150 per million tokens.
