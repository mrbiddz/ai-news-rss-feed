Mr. AI Releases Cost-Effective AI Model

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Mr. AI released a new AI model with competitive pricing for developers using their API: 40 cents per million input tokens and $2 per million output tokens. This pricing is comparable to GPT-4.1 mini, which costs $2 per million input tokens and $8 per million output tokens. According to benchmarks, the model performs well on code instruction following, math, knowledge, and long context, making it comparable to Llama 4 Maverick, GPT-4o, and Claude Sonnet 3.7.
