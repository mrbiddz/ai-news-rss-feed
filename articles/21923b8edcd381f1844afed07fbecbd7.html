LTX Studio Integrates Google's V3 Video Model

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

LTX Studio has integrated Google's V3 AI video model into their all-in-one video production platform. V3 can generate sound effects along with video and is considered one of the most powerful generative video models available. LTX Studio allows users to create full shot-by-shot storyboards that turn into animations, with the ability to inject themselves or others into videos through face training.
