GPT-5 Rumored for August Release

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Rumors suggest that OpenAI's GPT-5 will be released in August. While Sam Altman has confirmed on X that GPT-5 is coming soon, he didn't provide a specific date. Anonymous sources familiar with OpenAI are claiming the August timeframe.
