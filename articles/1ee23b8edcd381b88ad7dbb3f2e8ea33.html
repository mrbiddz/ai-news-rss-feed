Higsfield AI Introduces Effects Mix for Video Creation

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Higsfield AI launched Effects Mix, a feature that allows users to apply and blend pre-built effects to images to create videos. Similar to Pika Effects, users can select from various effects like turning metal, melting, set on fire, thunder god, agent reveal, glam, and soul jump. These effects can be combined to create unique animations, such as a character that catches on fire or a soul jumping out of a body.
