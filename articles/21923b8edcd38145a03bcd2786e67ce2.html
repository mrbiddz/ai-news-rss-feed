Microsoft and OpenAI Relationship Reportedly Strained

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

The relationship between Microsoft and OpenAI appears to be deteriorating. A point of contention involves OpenAI's acquisition of Windsurf, with OpenAI reportedly not wanting Microsoft (which owns 49% of OpenAI) to have equity in Windsurf because Microsoft runs GitHub Copilot, a competitor to Windsurf's products.
