Argil Introduces AI Avatars That Can Hold Products

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Argil has introduced a new feature where AI-generated avatars can hold up actual products. This could be significant for e-commerce companies, allowing them to create AI spokespeople who can hold and demonstrate products. Examples shown include avatars holding products, podcasting, and cooking. The presenter notes that this technology could allow companies to create their own mascots or spokespeople for brand promotion.
