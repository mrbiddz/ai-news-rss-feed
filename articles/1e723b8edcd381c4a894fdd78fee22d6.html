Aurora Deploys Driverless Trucks in Texas

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

After years of testing and validation, Aurora has deployed fully autonomous tractor-trailers on public highways in Texas. The company's Class 8 trucks are now making customer deliveries between Dallas and Houston without human drivers, having already completed 1,200 miles autonomously.
