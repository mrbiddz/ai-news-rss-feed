Figure's Helix Robot Demonstrates Laundry Capabilities

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Figure Robotics founder Brett Adcock demonstrated their Helix robot performing laundry tasks, showing progress toward household robots that can handle everyday chores, though it currently focuses on loading rather than the more complex tasks of unloading and folding.
