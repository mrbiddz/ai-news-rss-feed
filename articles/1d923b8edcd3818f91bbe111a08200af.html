Google Expands V2 Video Generation Availability

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Google rolled out V2 video generation to more platforms, including direct integration in Gemini Advanced and Whisk. Gemini Advanced users can now generate videos from text prompts within the chat interface. V2 is also available for developers through the Gemini API. Additionally, Google is offering college students in the US free access to Gemini Advanced, Notebook LM Plus, and 2TB of storage for the current and next school year.
