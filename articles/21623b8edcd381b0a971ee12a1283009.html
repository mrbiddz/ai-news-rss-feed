Efficiency Challenges in AI Development

Published: Thu, 19 Jun 2025

Microsoft is focusing on making AI more efficient and accessible. Kevin Scott discusses the importance of reducing costs, improving energy efficiency, and finding disruptive breakthroughs like the transformer technology. The goal is to eventually host powerful AI models locally on personal devices. Microsoft is also working with the electric power industry to find sustainable energy sources, potentially solving other problems like water scarcity through cheaper desalination if energy becomes more abundant.
