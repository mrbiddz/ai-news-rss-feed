New Large Language Models Released: Deepseek 3.1, Seed OSS 36B, and Neotron Nano 9B v2

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Three new large language models were released this week: Deepseek 3.1 with 685 billion parameters and a 128,000 token context window; Seed OSS 36B from ByteDance (TikTok's parent company) with a 512,000 token context window; and Nvidia's Neotron Nano 9B version 2 with toggleable reasoning capabilities and an enterprise-friendly license allowing commercial use and derivative models.
