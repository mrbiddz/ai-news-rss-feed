AI Coming to Android Ecosystem

Published: Sat, 17 May 2025

Google announced that Gemini is coming to the entire Android ecosystem. The AI assistant will be available on Wear OS smartwatches later this year, allowing for AI conversations directly from your wrist. It's also coming to Android Auto, enabling hands-free conversational assistance while driving, with features like summarizing and translating text messages, discussing current events, or providing personalized news digests. Additionally, Gemini will be available on Google TV later this year to help with content recommendations and other conversational features.
