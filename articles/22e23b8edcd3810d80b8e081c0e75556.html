AI Helps Couple Conceive After 18 Years of Trying

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

A couple who had been trying to conceive for 18 years successfully became pregnant with the help of AI. Using a method called the STAR method, AI technology was able to identify viable sperm cells that were previously undetectable, allowing for successful fertilization of an egg.
