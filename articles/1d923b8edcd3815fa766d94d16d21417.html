Luma Dream Machine Adds Camera Angle Controls

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Luma Dream Machine added camera angle controls to their video generation tool. Users can now select from various camera movements including static, handheld, zoom in/out, pan left/right, tilt up/down, push in, pull out, truck left/right, and point-of-view shots to create more dynamic AI-generated videos.
