Higsfield AI Releases Soul Image Model

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Higsfield AI released a new image model called Soul that creates super realistic images. The model appears to be designed with various presets in mind, such as hair clips and elevator mirror selfies. Currently, it seems to only support text-to-image generation.
