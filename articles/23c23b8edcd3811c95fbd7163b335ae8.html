DeepMind Develops Tool for Ancient Text Analysis

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Google DeepMind showcased research called Anas (or Anus) that transforms how historians connect with the past. The model contextualizes ancient inscriptions, helps historians interpret them, and can restore fragmentary texts.
