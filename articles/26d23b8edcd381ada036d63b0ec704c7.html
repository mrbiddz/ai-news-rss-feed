New LLM Updates: Grock, Google, Alibaba, and BYU

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Several new large language models have been released: Grock Code Fast 1 for coding, Google's Embedding Gemma (a small open-source model for on-device use), Alibaba's Quinn3 Next 80B A3B (in two flavors), and BYU's Ernie X1.1 reasoning model, which reportedly performs on par with GPT-5 and Gemini 2.5 Pro with superior accuracy and low hallucination rates.
