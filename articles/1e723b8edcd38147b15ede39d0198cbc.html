Lyft Launches AI Earnings Assistant for Drivers

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Lyft introduced an AI earnings assistant to help drivers optimize their income. The tool allows drivers to ask how to optimize their shifts based on real-time data about airport arrivals, local events, and other factors that affect ride demand, helping them maximize earnings by being in the right place at the right time.
