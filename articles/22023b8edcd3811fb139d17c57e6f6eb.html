Ring Cameras Getting AI-Powered Video Descriptions

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Ring cameras are getting an AI upgrade with a new feature called video descriptions. This feature generates detailed descriptions of what Ring cameras see and delivers them in notifications. Instead of simple alerts like 'motion at your front,' users will receive more detailed descriptions such as 'two people walking on the driveway with a white dog.'
