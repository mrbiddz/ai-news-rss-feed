DeepMind CEO Discusses AI Self-Awareness on 60 Minutes

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Demis Hassabis, CEO of DeepMind, appeared on 60 Minutes and discussed the possibility of AI developing self-awareness. While he stated that self-awareness is not an explicit goal, he acknowledged that 'it may happen implicitly' and that AI systems might acquire 'some feeling of self-awareness.' He noted that it's important for these systems to understand 'you, self, and other,' which could be 'the beginning of something like self-awareness.'
