XAI Co-Founder Igor Babuschkin Departs

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Igor Babuschkin, one of the co-founders of XAI (Elon Musk's AI company), announced his departure from the company. He led the engineering teams and helped build the startup into one of Silicon Valley's leading AI model developers. Elon Musk responded to his announcement saying, 'Thanks for helping build XAI. We wouldn't be here without you.' Babuschkin is moving into venture capital, founding Babuschkin Ventures.
