MidJourney Updates HD Video Generation Access

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

MidJourney has updated its service to allow HD video generation on standard subscriptions, not just higher-tier plans. Users on lower-end plans can now generate HD videos, though they're limited to smaller batch video jobs (one or two videos at a time). Other updates include separating mood boards onto their own page, changing thumbnails on video jobs, and improving video moderation.
