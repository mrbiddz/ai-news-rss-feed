Microsoft Attempts to Poach Meta AI Talent

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Microsoft is reportedly trying to poach AI talent from Meta, offering multi-million dollar pay packages. This is happening despite Meta and Microsoft working closely together. Microsoft is offering eight and nine-figure deals to attract talent from Meta, similar to how Meta previously poached talent from OpenAI and Google.
