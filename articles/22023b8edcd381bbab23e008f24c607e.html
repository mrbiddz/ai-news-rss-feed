Google DeepMind Releases Alpha Genome

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Google DeepMind released Alpha Genome, an AI model that can read a million DNA letters at once and predict in detail what happens when a mutation occurs. This tool could reshape understanding of human disease and accelerate breakthroughs in genetics and medicine. Google has provided access to scientists through its API for non-commercial research.
