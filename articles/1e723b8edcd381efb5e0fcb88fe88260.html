Duolingo Announces AI-First Approach

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Following Shopify's lead, Duolingo announced they're becoming an AI-first company. They plan to gradually stop using contractors for work that AI can handle, stating they need to rethink much of how they work rather than making minor tweaks to existing systems. Duolingo emphasized that this isn't about replacing employees but removing bottlenecks and allowing staff to focus on creative work and real problems instead of repetitive tasks.
