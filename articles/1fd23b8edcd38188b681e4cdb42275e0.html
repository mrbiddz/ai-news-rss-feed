Google's Other AI Announcements at IO

Published: Sun, 25 May 2025

Google made numerous additional AI announcements at IO, including: Imagine 4, an improved image model with better text rendering and realism; Google Beam (formerly Project Starline) for 3D video conferencing; Android XR and AR glasses with real-time translation capabilities; AI mode in Google Search with deep search and multi-question capabilities; virtual try-on technology for clothing; Gemini integration across Google products; Gemini 2.5 Pro and Flash models with improved performance; Deep Think mode enabling models to consider multiple hypotheses; Gemma 3N for mobile devices; Jewels asynchronous coding agent; and upcoming video overviews in Notebook LM.
