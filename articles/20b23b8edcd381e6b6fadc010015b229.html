Suno updates AI music platform with new features

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

Suno, considered one of the best AI music generation platforms, released an update with several new features. Users can now reorder, rewrite, and remake tracks section by section, extract specific stems (vocals, drums, bass, guitar, piano), upload longer songs up to eight minutes, and enjoy improved creative controls.
