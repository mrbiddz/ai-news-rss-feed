Tavis Releases New Lip-Syncing Model

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Tavis has released a new lip-syncing model that is supposedly the best available. While the presenter notes that AI voice with lip sync still feels somewhat unnatural, the technology is improving rapidly. The demo shows examples including a Donald Trump simulation, which still looks slightly uncanny but represents significant progress in the technology.
