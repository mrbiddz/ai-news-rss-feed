Amazon Launches Amazon Lens Live Feature

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Amazon has introduced Amazon Lens Live, a feature in the Amazon app that allows users to scan real-world items with their camera. The app then attempts to find the same or similar items available on Amazon, making it easier to add products to wishlists or make purchases after seeing them in person.
