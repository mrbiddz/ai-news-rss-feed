Nano Banana: Google's Powerful Image Editing Model

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Nano Banana is a powerful AI image editing model believed to be from Google. It can perform precise edits like changing specific objects in images while maintaining the rest of the image intact, combining multiple images naturally, and selectively applying style changes. Currently accessible through LM Arena's battle mode, users may need multiple attempts to get the model. Google executives have hinted at their involvement with cryptic banana-related posts.
