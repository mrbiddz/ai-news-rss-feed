Google Announces AI-Powered Finance and Flight Tools

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Google announced a new AI-powered Google Finance tool that will allow users to research finance questions with AI, offering advanced charting tools and real-time data and news. Additionally, Google is rolling out an AI update to its flight deals tool that will surface relevant, affordable flight options and allow users to describe travel ideas in natural language. Both features are being rolled out over the coming weeks in the US, with the flight tool also available in Canada and India.
