ChatGPT Brings Back GPT-4 After User Feedback

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Following the launch of GPT-5 and user feedback about the removal of GPT-4, OpenAI has put GPT-4 back into ChatGPT. Users who preferred GPT-4 over GPT-5 can now go back to using their preferred model.
