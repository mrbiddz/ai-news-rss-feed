Google's Made by Google Event Showcases AI-Powered Devices

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Google's annual Made by Google event revealed several AI-powered devices. The Pixel 10 phone features the Tensor G5 chip co-designed with Google DeepMind for on-device AI, including Magic Q which provides contextual assistance across apps. Other features include real-time voice translation, AI image editing, and photo improvement suggestions. Google also announced Pixel Buds 2A with Gemini integration, Pixel Watch 4 with an AI health coach, and Gemini for Home devices.
