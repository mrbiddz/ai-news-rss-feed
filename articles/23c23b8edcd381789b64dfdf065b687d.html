Delta Airlines Using AI for Dynamic Pricing

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Delta Airlines is implementing AI to set maximum prices customers are willing to pay, resulting in different prices for different customers based on what the AI determines their pricing threshold to be. This has raised concerns about fairness, as customers who are deemed able to afford more will be charged higher prices for the same seats.
