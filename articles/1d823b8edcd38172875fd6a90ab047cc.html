New AI Tools for Creators and Developers

Published: Thu, 17 Apr 2025

YouTube launched a free AI music-making tool for creators to generate royalty-free background music. DaVinci Resolve released version 20 with numerous AI features, including the ability to align video footage with scripts, improved magic mask, and AI voiceovers. Runway introduced Gen 4 Turbo, which can generate 10-second videos in just 30 seconds. Amazon unveiled Nova Reel, an AI video generator capable of creating videos up to 2 minutes long. For developers, Together AI announced Deep Coder 14B, an open-source coding model. Google made Gemini 2.5 Flash, Pro Live API, and V2 available in the Gemini API. Groq 3 now has an API, GitHub Copilot added an agent mode, and 11 Labs released an MCP server for easier integration with language models. WordPress launched an AI website builder, and Shopify's CEO announced they won't hire new employees unless they can prove AI can't do the job first.
