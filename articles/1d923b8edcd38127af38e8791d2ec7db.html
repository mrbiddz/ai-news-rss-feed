Apple and Google Working on AR Glasses

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Tim Cook reportedly aims to release Apple AR glasses before Meta launches their next version of Ray-Ban smart glasses with heads-up display. Meanwhile, Google demonstrated their AR glasses at TED talks, featuring capabilities like object recognition, translation, navigation with 3D maps, and a heads-up display. The presenter had previously tested these glasses at Google DeepMind in London and found them impressive, with potential announcements coming at Google I/O next month.
