Anthropic Introduces Integrations for Claude

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Anthropic announced integrations for Claude, allowing more official connections to Claude that will work inside the Claude web app. These integrations use Model Context Protocols (MCPs), which enable large language models to communicate with other apps. Currently, these new features are only available on the Max team and enterprise plans, not on the standard $20/month plan.
