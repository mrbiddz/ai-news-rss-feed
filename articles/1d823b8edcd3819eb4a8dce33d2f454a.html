Anthropic Introduces Max Plan and Announces Claude 4

Published: Thu, 17 Apr 2025

Anthropic rolled out a new Max Plan for Claude, priced at $100 per month, which provides five times more usage than the Pro plan. They also introduced a Maximum Flexibility plan at $200 per month with 20 times more usage than Pro. Jared Kaplan, Anthropic's Chief Scientist, announced that Claude 4 will be released within the next six months.
