Udio Launches Sessions Feature for AI Music

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Udio, a company that helps generate music with AI, rolled out a new tool called Sessions. This feature automatically identifies musical elements like choruses and bridges from audio waveforms, allowing for the editing of lyrics and sound through a visual workstation. Users can select different areas of songs and edit lyrics, though the interface is described as not being very intuitive.
