Lego GPT: Text-to-Lego AI Model

Published: Sat, 17 May 2025

Carnegie Mellon University unveiled Lego GPT, an AI model that converts text prompts into Lego building instructions. The model can create designs for objects like sofas, beds, mugs, chairs, and cameras. It was trained on a limited dataset of 21 object categories and is restricted to creating structures made of one-unit-tall cuboid bricks on a 20x20x20 grid. Researchers demonstrated that robots could follow the generated instructions to physically assemble the Lego creations. The model is available on Hugging Face, though it's currently quite slow and limited to specific object categories.
