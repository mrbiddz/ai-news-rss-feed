Microsoft Releases New AI Models: MAI Voice 1 and MAI1 Preview

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

Microsoft has released two new in-house AI models: MAI Voice 1, their first highly expressive natural speech generation model, and MAI1 Preview, a language model that uses a mixture of experts. MAI Voice 1 can be tested in Copilot Labs with different modes (emotive, story), various voices, and different emotional styles. The voice model produces natural-sounding speech but tends to add extra words and sentences beyond the provided script.
