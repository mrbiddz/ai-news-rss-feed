YouTube Announces AI Features at Made on YouTube Event

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

YouTube announced several AI features at its Made on YouTube event, including: Ask Studio (an AI bot for creators to analyze channel data), title A/B testing, auto-dubbing with lip sync to make translated videos appear natural, likeness detection to prevent deepfakes, a speech-to-song remixing tool using DeepMind's LIA 2 model, and integration of Runway's Gen-3 (V3) directly into YouTube Shorts for free video generation.
