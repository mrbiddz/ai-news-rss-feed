Nvidia Releases Fast Speech-to-Text Model

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Nvidia quietly released a new speech-to-text model that can transcribe 60 minutes of audio in just 1 second. The open-source model has an error rate of 6.05%, meaning it gets approximately six out of every 100 words wrong. Unlike OpenAI's Whisper or other speech-to-text models, users don't need to pay API fees. The model is available on Hugging Face for those who don't want to run it locally.
