Baby in Colombia Named 'Chat Yippity' After ChatGPT

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

A baby in Colombia has been registered with the name 'Chat Yippity,' inspired by the AI chatbot ChatGPT, marking an unusual example of AI's cultural impact reaching into naming conventions.
