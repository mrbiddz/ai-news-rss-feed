GPT-5 Expected This Summer

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

On the OpenAI podcast, Sam Altman indicated that GPT-5 will likely be released 'sometime this summer.' He also mentioned that OpenAI plans to simplify their naming conventions going forward, standardizing on sequential numbering (GPT-5, GPT-6, etc.) rather than the confusing mix of version numbers and suffixes currently in use.
