AI-Powered Robots and Gadgets

Published: Thu, 17 Apr 2025

Amazon's Zoox has begun rolling out robotaxis in Los Angeles. Samsung is finally releasing the Ballie, a ball-shaped robot that rolls around and projects content on the floor, which was first showcased at CES 2024. Kawasaki demonstrated Corio, a robot 'dog' designed to be ridden like a motorcycle or quad bike, though some of the footage shared was CGI rather than actual product demonstrations.
