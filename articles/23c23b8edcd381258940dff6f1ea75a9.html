Google Introduces Opal for Creating AI Mini Apps

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Google Labs introduced Opal, a tool that lets users describe, create, and share AI mini apps. Available now in the US during public beta, it allows users to create AI-powered workflows. Users can start with pre-built apps like blog post writers or book recommendation tools and remix them, or create new apps from scratch by describing what they want. The tool can automatically build workflows that can be manually edited if needed.
