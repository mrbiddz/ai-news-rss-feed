Tesla's Optimus Robot Shows Advanced Dancing Capabilities

Published: Sat, 17 May 2025

Elon Musk shared a video of Tesla's Optimus humanoid robot performing advanced dance moves. Initially shown tethered to a rail, additional footage revealed the robot can perform these movements untethered at normal speed. The videos demonstrate impressive human-like motion capabilities, though the practical applications beyond demonstration purposes remain unclear.
