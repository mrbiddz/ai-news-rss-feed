xAI's Grok Chatbot Gets Vision Capabilities

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

xAI has updated its Grok chatbot with vision capabilities, similar to what's available in Gemini and OpenAI models. The feature works on the Grok mobile app, allowing users to take pictures and have Grok analyze what it sees. The presenter tested this by having Grok identify objects in his workspace and a picture on the wall, noting that Grok 3 is an impressive model that doesn't get enough credit.
