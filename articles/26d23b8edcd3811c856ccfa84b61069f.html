Figure Robot Demonstrates Dishwashing Capabilities

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

The Figure humanoid robot has demonstrated its ability to wash dishes, adding to its previously showcased capabilities of loading washing machines and folding laundry. This represents progress toward robots that can handle common household chores.
