LTX Studio Adds Google's V2 Video Generation Model

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

LTX Studio has integrated Google's V2 video generation model into their platform, making it the least expensive way to generate videos with V2. Using LTX Studio costs about 65¢ per 8 seconds of video, compared to Google's own cloud platform which charges 50¢ per second. LTX Studio is becoming more model-agnostic, offering both their own open-source model LTXV and now Google's V2.
