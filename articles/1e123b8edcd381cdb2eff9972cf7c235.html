Character AI Rolls Out Video Generation Feature

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Character AI, which allows users to create fictional characters to chat with, has rolled out a new feature that generates videos of the characters. The 'avatar effects' feature generates visuals for the characters, making interactions feel more like talking with a character rather than just a text chatbot. The feature is currently being rolled out with early access available by application.
