OpenAI Makes Deep Research Available on Free Plan

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

OpenAI has made their Deep Research feature available on the free plan of ChatGPT. Free users get five uses per month, while Plus, Team, and Pro users get access to both the original Deep Research and a new lightweight version. The lightweight version is powered by a version of O4 Mini that's nearly as intelligent as the original Deep Research while being significantly cheaper to serve. Responses from the lightweight version will typically be shorter while maintaining depth and quality.
