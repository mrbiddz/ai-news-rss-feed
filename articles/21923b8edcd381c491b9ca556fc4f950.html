Halo AI Releases Three New AI Products

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

Chinese company Halo AI launched three new products: 1) Miniax M1, an open-source model with a 1 million token context window and 456 billion parameters; 2) Mini Max agent, a general intelligent agent for complex tasks that can break down projects into phases and steps; and 3) Halu O2, a new video model that excels at instruction following and handling complex physics like acrobatics and juggling.
