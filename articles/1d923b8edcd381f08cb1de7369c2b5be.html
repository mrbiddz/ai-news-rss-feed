Anthropic Updates Claude with Research Feature and Google Workspace Integration

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Anthropic added a research feature to Claude that connects with Google services to search Gmail, Calendar, Google Drive, and the web simultaneously. This feature is currently in early beta for Max Team and Enterprise plans. Google Workspace integrations are available to all paid users. Bloomberg reports that Anthropic is also preparing to launch a voice assistant called 'voice mode' for Claude as soon as this month, initially on a limited basis.
