Google's Veo Model Shows Emergent Text-Following Behavior

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Google discovered that their Veo video generation model has emergent behavior where it follows text instructions written directly on input images. Users can annotate an image with text describing what should happen in the video, and the model will follow those instructions when generating the video, though the text appears in the first few frames before fading out.
