Apple and Anthropic to Develop AI Coding Platform

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Apple and Anthropic are teaming up to build an AI coding platform, which will be a new version of Xcode (Apple's programming software for developing Apple products). The platform will integrate Anthropic's Claude Sonnet model, though details about the collaboration are still limited.
