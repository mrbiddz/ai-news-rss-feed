GPT-4o Image Generation Shows Drift Over Multiple Iterations

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Users have discovered that when repeatedly asking GPT-4o to create an exact replica of an image, the result gradually changes with each iteration. After dozens of repetitions (74-101 times in examples shared), the final image bears little resemblance to the original. This demonstrates how small changes accumulate over multiple generations, even when explicitly instructed to create exact replicas.
