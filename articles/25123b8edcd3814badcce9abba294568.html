Geoffrey Hinton Warns About AI Risks on CNN

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

AI pioneer Geoffrey Hinton appeared on CNN to discuss his concerns about artificial intelligence. He maintained his belief that there's a 10-20% chance of AIs wiping out humans if we can't solve the alignment problem. Hinton suggested that we need to train 'maternal instincts' into AI models to ensure they remain aligned with human values as they become smarter than humans, which he believes will happen within the next 5-20 years.
