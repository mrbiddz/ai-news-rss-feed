OpenAI to Release GPT-4o Mini and GPT-4o Before GPT-5

Published: Thu, 17 Apr 2025

Despite previous statements about skipping directly to GPT-5, Sam Altman announced that OpenAI will release GPT-4o and GPT-4o Mini before GPT-5. Altman indicated that while GPT-5 will be better than expected, it's taking longer to develop, so these intermediate models will be released in the meantime. OpenAI also introduced a new memory feature in ChatGPT that allows the chatbot to reference past conversations and tailor its answers based on previous interactions.
