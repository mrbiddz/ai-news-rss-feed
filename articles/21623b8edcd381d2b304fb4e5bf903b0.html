The Future of AI Agents in Healthcare

Published: Thu, 19 Jun 2025

Microsoft is making significant progress in healthcare applications of AI. CEO Satya Nadella highlighted a collaboration with Stanford University's tumor board, where AI agents are helping oncologists review cancer cases and determine treatment guidelines. This agentic AI orchestrator has reduced the lead time for diagnosing, analyzing, and developing treatment protocols from weeks to hours, demonstrating a real-world benefit of AI in healthcare settings.
