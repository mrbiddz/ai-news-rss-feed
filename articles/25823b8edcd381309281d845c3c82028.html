Runway ML Updates Chat Interface and Adds Third-Party Models

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Runway ML has updated their chat interface, keeping conversations on the left and generations on the right for better visibility. They've also added third-party models directly inside their chat mode, including V-O-3, and made Gen 4 image turbo mode available to all users for faster image generation.
