Gemini 2.0 Adds Image Creation and Editing Capabilities

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Google's Gemini 2.0 now allows developers to create and edit images through its API. The functionality is similar to GPT-4o's image model, enabling users to manipulate images by adding or moving objects between pictures. The feature is available in Google AI Studio, where users can generate images with simple prompts like 'create an image of a wolf howling at the moon' or make edits like 'put sunglasses on the moon.'
