New LLM Models Released

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Several new large language models were released: Google's improved Gemini 2.5 Flash and Flash Light, XAI's Grok 4 Fast (a faster version of Grok 4), Alibaba's Qwen3 Max (strong at coding and agentic skills), and Qwen 3 Omni (a multimodal model handling text, image, audio, and video).
