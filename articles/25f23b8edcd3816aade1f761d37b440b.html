Sync Labs Releases LipSync 2 Pro for Video Dialogue Editing

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

Sync Labs has launched LipSync 2 Pro, a video model that can edit what people say in videos by changing both the audio and lip movements to match new dialogue. While functional, the technology still struggles with facial hair like beards and mustaches, which can make the results look somewhat unnatural.
