OpenAI Introduces GPTOSS Open Weight Models

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

OpenAI has released GPTOSS, their open weight models that can be run locally or in the cloud without sharing data back to OpenAI. They've released two versions: GPTOSS 120B and GPTOSS 20B. These models are comparable in power to the previous generation before GPT-5 and are available for users to build upon, iterate, and fine-tune as desired. The models can be used completely free, don't require internet once downloaded, and don't send any data anywhere since they can run offline and locally.
