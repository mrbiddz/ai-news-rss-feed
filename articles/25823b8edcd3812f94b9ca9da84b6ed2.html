OpenAI Updates GPT-5 and Hints at GPT-6

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

OpenAI has updated GPT-5 to be warmer and friendlier based on user feedback, adding small touches like 'good question' and 'great start.' Meanwhile, Sam Altman has hinted that GPT-6 is coming faster than expected, with enhanced memory capabilities being a key focus. The goal is to create a more personalized AI that remembers user preferences, routines, and quirks to provide tailored responses.
