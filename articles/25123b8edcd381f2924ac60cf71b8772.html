ChatGPT's GPT-3.5 Wins Google's Chess Tournament

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

In Google's Kaggle chess tournament pitting AI models against each other, ChatGPT's GPT-3.5 model emerged as the winner. It defeated Grok 4 in the finals after both models had advanced by beating other competitors. GPT-5 was not included in the competition. Other notable results included GPT-3.5 beating GPT-4 Mini, Grok 4 beating Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Gemini 2.5 defeating Claude Opus 4.
