AI Tool Detects Heart Disease from Existing CT Scans

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

Researchers from Mass General Brigham and the US Department of Veterans Affairs have developed an AI tool that can detect signs of heart disease using existing CT scans in patients' medical records. The tool identifies high coronary artery calcium that indicates greater risk for cardiovascular events, potentially allowing for early detection without requiring additional scans.
