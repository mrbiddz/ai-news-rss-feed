Nvidia Launches Version 4.0 with AI Twin Feature

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

Nvidia has launched version 4.0 with a new AI Twin feature that allows users to upload videos of themselves to create digital clones or use pre-created avatars. Users can create twins from YouTube videos, record fresh content, or clone just their voice. The feature enables users to generate videos with their AI twin speaking custom content. Nvidia's technology is useful for last-minute re-shoots, creating simple ads, or just experimenting with the technology. The feature is available at nvidia.io.
