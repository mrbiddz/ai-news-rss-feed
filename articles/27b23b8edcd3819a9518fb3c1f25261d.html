Meta Introduces Ray-Ban Smart Glasses with Display

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Meta has announced Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses with a built-in display on one lens. The glasses feature a neural wristband that detects hand gestures for control, allowing users to swipe through apps and adjust volume with intuitive movements. Features include viewing WhatsApp messages, real-time translation with subtitles, and a focus mode that isolates conversations in noisy environments.
