Elon Musk and Sam Altman Feud Over App Store Rankings

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Elon Musk accused Apple of anticompetitive behavior, claiming they make it impossible for any AI company besides OpenAI to reach the top of the App Store. He threatened legal action. Sam Altman responded by suggesting Musk manipulates X (formerly Twitter) algorithms to benefit his companies and harm competitors. The public exchange continued with Musk calling Altman a liar and Altman challenging Musk to sign an affidavit that he has never directed changes to X's algorithm to benefit himself.
