MidJourney Introduces HD Video Mode

Published: Sun, 10 Aug 2025

MidJourney has rolled out an HD video mode for Pro and Mega subscribers. The new feature is 3.2 times more expensive to generate than standard definition video but provides four times as many pixels. This higher-end video option is now available to users on MidJourney's premium subscription plans.
