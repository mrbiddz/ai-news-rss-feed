Descript Testing Agentic AI Video Editing Features

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Descript is testing new AI agentic features for video editing, described as 'the cursor for AI video editing.' In demos, users can prompt the AI with requests like 'Make a one-minute video with my top three tips on seeming natural on camera' or 'Can you edit this down?' and the AI will write scripts, make edits, add chapter titles, insert stock overlays, and even mask jump cuts with subtle zooms. The feature isn't publicly available yet, but represents a significant step toward agentic AI video editing.
