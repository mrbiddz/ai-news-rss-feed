HeyGen Introduces Product Placement Feature

Published: Sun, 22 Jun 2025

HeyGen has launched a new feature called 'product placement' that allows users to upload a product photo, choose an avatar, and add a script to create an ad where the person appears to be holding and talking about the product. The feature can use text-to-speech or lip-sync to recorded audio. While currently not photorealistic enough to fool viewers, this raises questions about potential future misuse with celebrity images for fake endorsements.
