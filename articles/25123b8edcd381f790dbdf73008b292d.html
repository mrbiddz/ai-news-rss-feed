XAI Makes Grok 4 Free Worldwide

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

In response to GPT-5's release, XAI has made Grok 4 free for all users worldwide. Previously, Grok 4 was only available on paid plans, with older Grok models being free. This move appears to be a direct response to OpenAI making GPT-5 available in the free version of ChatGPT.
