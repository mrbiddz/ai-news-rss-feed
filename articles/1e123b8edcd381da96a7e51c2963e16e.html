Adobe Releases New Version of Firefly

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Adobe released a new version of Firefly in their web app and added the ability to choose between different models. Users can now select from Firefly Image 4, Firefly Image 4 Ultra, Imagine 3, and GPT Image. The presenter tested the new models with a 'wolf howling at the moon' prompt and found that the standard Firefly Image 4 produced better results than the Ultra version.
