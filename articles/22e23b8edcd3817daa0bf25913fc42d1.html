Moon Valley Releases Ethical AI Video Model

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

Moon Valley has released an AI video model specifically designed for filmmakers that's trained entirely on openly licensed data. This approach aims to help filmmakers avoid potential lawsuits over AI-generated content that might resemble copyrighted material. The model offers various control features including motion transfer, pose transfer, camera control, trajectory control, and shot extension. While generation times are somewhat slow (around 5 minutes), the ethical training approach may appeal to users concerned about the source of training data.
