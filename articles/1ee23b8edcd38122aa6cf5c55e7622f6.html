Netflix Testing AI-Powered Search Features

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Netflix is introducing AI upgrades for subscribers, including a new search feature that uses generative AI to allow members to search for shows and movies using natural conversational phrases like 'I want something funny and upbeat.' The feature is currently available as a small opt-in beta on iOS. Additionally, Netflix will be testing a vertical feed filled with clips of shows and movies to make discovery easier, similar to TikTok's format.
