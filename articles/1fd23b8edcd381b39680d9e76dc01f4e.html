Anthropic Introduces Claude 4

Published: Sun, 25 May 2025

Anthropic introduced Claude 4 models, including Opus 4 and Sonnet 4, which outperform competitors in software engineering benchmarks. The company appears to be focusing on making the best reasoning and coding models rather than competing directly with general-purpose chatbots. Claude 4 is a hybrid model that can operate with or without chain-of-thought reasoning. Despite initial reports suggesting the model might contact authorities about unethical user requests, Anthropic researcher Sam Bowman clarified this only occurs in specific testing environments with unusual access and instructions.
