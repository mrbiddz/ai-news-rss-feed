Open Art Launches One-Click Story Feature

Published: Sun, 17 Aug 2025

Open Art, founded by former Google employees, released a new feature called 'one-click story' that creates AI-generated 'brain rot' videos. Users can input a single sentence, script, or song and turn it into a one-minute video with a story arc. The feature offers options for character vlogs, music videos, explainer videos, or creating from scratch. However, the free credits provided don't cover the cost of generating a video, requiring users to subscribe to their $14/month plan.
