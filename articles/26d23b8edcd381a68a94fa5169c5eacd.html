OpenAI's V3 Updates: Vertical Videos and Price Cuts

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

OpenAI's V3 video generation model now supports vertical video creation. The company has also significantly reduced pricing, with V3 costs cut almost in half and V3 Fast costs reduced by more than half. YouTube has confirmed V3 integration is coming to YouTube Shorts later this summer, and the technology is now available in Google Photos for animating still images.
