Midjourney Partners with Meta for Image Generation

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

In an unexpected partnership, Midjourney is collaborating with Meta to license their aesthetic technology for Meta's future models and products. This is particularly surprising as Midjourney has historically avoided integrations and partnerships, with no API available and usage limited to Discord or their website. The partnership suggests Meta may be shifting away from developing their own image models (like Emu) in favor of integrating established technologies.
