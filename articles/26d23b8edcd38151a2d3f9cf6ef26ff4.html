Meta Invests $140 Million in Black Forest Labs

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Meta is paying $140 million to use Black Forest Labs for AI image generation, despite recently partnering with Midjourney for similar capabilities. The dual investment suggests Meta wants both Black Forest Labs' ultra-realistic image generation and Midjourney's distinctive aesthetic style.
