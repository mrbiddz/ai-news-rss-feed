Anthropic Wins Key Ruling on AI Training

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Anthropic won a key ruling that determined training AI on legally purchased books falls under fair use. The company claimed they bought books, scanned them, and used them to train their models, which the judge ruled was permissible. However, Anthropic is still facing scrutiny over potentially using pirated books in their training data.
