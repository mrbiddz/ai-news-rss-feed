Higsfield AI Launches Iconic Scenes Feature

Published: Sat, 03 May 2025

Higsfield AI released a new feature called Iconic Scenes that allows users to insert themselves into legendary movie moments using just a selfie. Users can select from various animated movie scenes and upload their own image to be integrated into the scene. While the feature is available on the free plan, generation times can be very slow.
