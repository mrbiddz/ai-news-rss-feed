Evolution of Jobs in the AI Era

Published: Thu, 19 Jun 2025

Kevin Scott and OpenAI's CPO Kevin Wheel both predict that jobs won't disappear but will transform significantly with AI. Wheel compared the situation to how rocket science evolved after computers took over calculations - the discipline remained important but changed dramatically. Scott envisions a future where AI agents reliably take action on our behalf, transitioning from synchronous interactions to systems that can independently complete tasks. Both executives express optimism about AI creating a faster, more efficient world with new opportunities.
