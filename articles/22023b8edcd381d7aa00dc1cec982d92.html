AI Model Diagnoses Brain Tumors Without Surgery

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Scientists in Berlin have developed an AI model called CrossN that can diagnose brain tumors with over 99% accuracy without requiring surgery. The system reads subtle DNA signals in fluid samples like spinal taps and can identify over 170 types of cancer. It's faster, safer, and more precise than traditional biopsies. Clinical trials are already being prepared in Germany.
