Google Translate Adds AI-Powered Live Translation and Language Learning

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

Google has added AI-powered live translation to its Translate app, allowing real-time communication between people speaking different languages. Additionally, they've introduced a new language learning feature that creates personalized scenarios and exercises based on the user's proficiency level, positioning Google as a direct competitor to language learning apps like Duolingo.
