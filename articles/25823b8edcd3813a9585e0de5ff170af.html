Google Updates AI Mode in Search with Agentic Features

Published: Sun, 24 Aug 2025

Google has updated their AI mode in search with more agentic features. Users can now make complex queries with multiple constraints, such as finding restaurants with specific requirements for party size, date, time, location, and cuisine preferences. The feature needs to be enabled through Google Labs by turning on AI mode.
