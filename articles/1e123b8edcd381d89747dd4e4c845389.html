Ray-Ban Meta Glasses Get Live Translation Feature

Published: Sun, 27 Apr 2025

Ray-Ban Meta glasses have rolled out a new live translation feature that allows users to hear real-time translations through the glasses' built-in headphones. For example, someone could speak to the user in Spanish, and they would hear an English translation in their ear. Users can also download language packs in advance to use the feature without internet access, addressing a previous limitation of the glasses when used in areas with poor connectivity.
