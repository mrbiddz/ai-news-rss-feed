Google Introduces Dolphin Gemma for Dolphin Communication Research

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Google unveiled Dolphin Gemma, a large language model designed to study dolphin communication. The model is trained to learn the structure of dolphin vocalizations and generate dolphin-like sound sequences. It's part of Google's open Gemma series of models, allowing researchers to build upon it for interspecies communication research.
