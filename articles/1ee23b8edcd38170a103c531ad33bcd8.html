OpenAI Releases Guide on When to Use Each Model

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

OpenAI published a guide called 'When to use each model' to help users understand the differences between their various models. GPT-4o excels at everyday tasks and is recommended as the default model for most scenarios. GPT-4.5 (which will be discontinued soon) is best for emotional intelligence and clear communication. GPT-4o Mini and Mini High are optimized for quick STEM queries and advanced coding respectively. GPT-3.5 is ideal for complex multi-step tasks and tends to create tables in its responses. GPT-3.5 Pro Mode is designed for complex reasoning tasks but is only available on the $200/month plan.
