Runway Introduces ALF for AI Video Editing

Published: Sun, 03 Aug 2025

Runway released a new feature called ALF that allows users to edit specific elements in videos using natural language instructions. Users can change environments (like putting a jet in space), replace objects (like replacing babies with a sandwich), and create new camera angles. The feature is available for all paid Runway accounts and works best when used in chat mode.
