AI World Models Will Enable Advanced Robotics

Published: Thu, 12 Jun 2025

Demis Hassabis discussed the long-term goal of creating AI with 'world models' that can understand not just language but also audio, images, video, and other inputs. He explained that for AI to be a good assistant or for robotics to work effectively in the real world, systems need to understand the physical context and environment. According to Hassabis, what's holding back robotics today isn't hardware but software intelligence. The development of accurate world models will make assistant technology like Project Astra and Gemini Live work more robustly, enabling AI to better understand what's happening in the physical world.
