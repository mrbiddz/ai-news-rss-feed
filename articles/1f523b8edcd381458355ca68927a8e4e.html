ChatGPT Updates: GPT-4.1 and PDF Export

Published: Sat, 17 May 2025

OpenAI has made the GPT-4.1 model, previously only available via API, directly accessible within ChatGPT for paid subscribers. GPT-4.1 excels at code generation, making it ideal for users who want to write code directly in the ChatGPT interface. OpenAI also rolled out a feature allowing users to export documents as well-formatted PDFs after conducting research in ChatGPT. Additionally, Windsurf (recently acquired by OpenAI for $3 billion) is launching their own model called SWE1 (Software Engineer 1), which comes in three versions: SWE1 (comparable to Claude 3.5 Sonnet), SWE1 Light, and SWE1 Mini. These models are available to all paid Windsurf users and use zero credits per prompt.
