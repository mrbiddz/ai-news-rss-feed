Luma Labs Releases Ray 3 Video Model

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Luma Labs has released Ray 3, described as the world's first reasoning video model. It claims to be the first to generate studio-grade HDR (high dynamic range) video and includes a draft mode for quick previews before generating higher quality versions. The model demonstrates improved physics understanding and can self-correct issues in generated videos, showing agentic capabilities by checking and improving its own work.
