Perplexity Launches Email Assistant

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Perplexity has launched an email assistant that organizes emails into categories like "to respond," "notification," "FYI," or "meeting update." It includes an AI composer for writing emails, calendar integration for scheduling assistance, and daily summaries of meetings and tasks. The feature is currently available only on Perplexity's $200/month Ultra plan.
