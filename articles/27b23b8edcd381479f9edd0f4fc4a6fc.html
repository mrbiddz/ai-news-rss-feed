Neon App Pays Users to Share Phone Calls for AI Training

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

An app called Neon pays users to share their phone conversations for AI training data. Users can earn about 30 cents per minute (up to $30 per day) by making calls through the app, which records conversations with consent. The app has become the seventh most downloaded iPhone app despite privacy concerns.
