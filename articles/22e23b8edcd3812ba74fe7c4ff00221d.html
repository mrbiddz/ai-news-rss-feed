Sakana AI Introduces TreeQuest for Multi-Model Collaboration

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

Sakana AI has unveiled TreeQuest, a new technique that allows multiple large language models to cooperate on a single task. The approach creates a team of AI agents that work together to solve problems that might be difficult for any single model. In testing, they combined O4 Mini, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Deepseek R1 to work collaboratively. The technology has been released as open-source on GitHub, representing a potential future direction for AI where multiple models collaborate rather than users having to choose between different models.
