Amazon Introduces Vulcan Robot with Sense of Touch

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

Amazon introduced Vulcan, its first robot with a sense of touch. This tactile capability allows the robot to determine how much pressure to apply when handling different objects, making it more effective for warehouse operations. The robot can be gentle with fragile items like glass and apply more pressure to heavier, less breakable objects, potentially speeding up package processing in Amazon warehouses.
