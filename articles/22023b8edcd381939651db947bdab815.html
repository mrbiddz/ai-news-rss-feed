US Government Proposes Blocking State AI Regulations

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

The US government is attempting to prevent states from regulating AI for the next 10 years by withholding federal broadband funding from states that try to enforce AI regulations. The proposal has opposition from both Democrats and Republicans. The motivation appears to be concern about falling behind in the AI race with countries like China, though setting such a long timeframe is being questioned.
