Amazon's Zoox Launching Robo-Taxis in Las Vegas

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Zoox, owned by Amazon, is preparing to launch autonomous robo-taxis in Las Vegas. These vehicles, which resemble small buses without steering wheels or pedals, will shuttle people along the Las Vegas Strip, potentially competing with Elon Musk's underground Hyperloop system.
