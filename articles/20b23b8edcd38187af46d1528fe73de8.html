11 Labs releases version 3 with improved voice capabilities

Published: Sun, 08 Jun 2025

11 Labs rolled out version 3 of their voice technology with enhanced features including the ability to create realistic conversations and add emotional elements like chuckles, laughter, excitement, and shouting. The platform offers both quick voice training with 30 seconds of audio and higher quality training with 30 minutes of audio.
