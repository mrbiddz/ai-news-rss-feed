OpenAI to Acquire Windsurf for $3 Billion

Published: Sat, 10 May 2025

According to Bloomberg, OpenAI has reached an agreement to purchase Windsurf for $3 billion. This acquisition has sparked discussion about OpenAI's timeline for achieving AGI (Artificial General Intelligence), with some questioning why OpenAI would invest in an IDE if they believe AGI is imminent.
