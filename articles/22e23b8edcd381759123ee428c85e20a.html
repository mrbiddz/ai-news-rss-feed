YouTube Clarifies Policy on AI-Generated Content

Published: Sun, 13 Jul 2025

YouTube is updating its restrictions around inauthentic content under the YouTube Partner Program guidelines, which some interpreted as plans to demonetize AI-generated content. However, YouTube creator liaison Renee Richie clarified that this is a minor update to long-standing policies aimed at better identifying mass-produced or repetitive content that has already been ineligible for monetization for years. The policy appears to target content that viewers consider spam rather than specifically targeting AI-generated videos.
