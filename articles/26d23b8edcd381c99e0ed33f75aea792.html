Apple Debuts AirPods Pro 3 with Live Translation

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

At Apple's annual event, they introduced AirPods Pro 3 with a groundbreaking live translation feature. The earbuds can translate conversations in real-time, allowing two people wearing AirPods to speak in their native languages while hearing translations in their preferred language. The feature is launching in beta with select languages.
