Google Vids Gets Major Updates with AI Video Integration

Published: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

Google's Vids platform has received significant updates, including integration with Google's VO3 generative video technology. Users can now add AI-generated video clips and animations to their presentation-style videos. Other new features include AI-generated avatars with lip-syncing capabilities and transcript-based video editing similar to Descript. While basic Vids editor is available to all consumers, the advanced AI features require a paid subscription to Google AI Pro, Ultra, or certain Workspace accounts.
