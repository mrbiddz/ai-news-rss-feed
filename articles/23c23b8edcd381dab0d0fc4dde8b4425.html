Leonardo Launches V3 Fast for Quicker, Cheaper Video Generation

Published: Sun, 27 Jul 2025

Leonardo has rolled out V3 Fast, which is three times faster than the original V3 for AI video generation. It can still generate 8-second videos with sound but is faster and cheaper to use. V3 Fast costs 2,000 credits compared to 2,500 for regular V3, though there is some loss in realism.
