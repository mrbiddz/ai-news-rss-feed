AI Upscaling Limitations in Criminal Investigations

Published: Sun, 14 Sep 2025

Following attempts to use AI to upscale low-resolution images of a suspect in a high-profile case, experts caution that AI upscalers don't reveal actual details but rather generate their best guess of what details might be. Different upscalers can produce significantly different results from the same source image, as the AI is hallucinating details that weren't present in the original.
