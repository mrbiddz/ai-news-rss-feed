Microsoft CTO Kevin Scott on AI's Impact on Programming Jobs

Published: Thu, 19 Jun 2025

Kevin Scott, CTO of Microsoft, shares his contrarian view that AI will actually create more programming jobs rather than eliminate them. He explains that while AI will handle the heavy lifting of coding, there will be an increased need for 'full stack developers' who understand systems from top to bottom and can debug AI when it misbehaves. The role of software engineers will evolve into more of a caretaker position, changing how the job is performed rather than eliminating it entirely.
