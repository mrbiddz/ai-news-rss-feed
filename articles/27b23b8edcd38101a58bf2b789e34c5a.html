Google Launches Mixboard

Published: Sun, 28 Sep 2025

Google has released Mixboard, a tool for creating mood boards and vision boards. Currently in public beta in the US, it allows users to upload images, generate new ones, and blend images together. The tool functions similarly to Halo AI's board feature but with less emphasis on agentic capabilities.
