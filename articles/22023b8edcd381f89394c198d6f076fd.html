Google DeepMind Launches Gemini Robotics On-Device

Published: Sun, 29 Jun 2025

Google DeepMind released Gemini Robotics on-device, an AI system for robots that runs entirely on the robot itself without needing internet connection. It allows robots to follow natural language commands, learn new skills from a few examples, and perform delicate tasks like folding clothes or assembling machinery.
