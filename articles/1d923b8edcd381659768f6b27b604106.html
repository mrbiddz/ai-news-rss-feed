Arcads.ai Introduces Gesture Control for AI Actors

Published: Sat, 19 Apr 2025

Arcads.ai launched a new feature allowing users to prompt AI actors to generate specific expressions and gestures like crying, laughing, celebrating, and pointing. The service uses images of real actors rather than fully AI-generated faces, requiring careful consideration of what users make them say or do. The service starts at $110/month for 10 videos.
